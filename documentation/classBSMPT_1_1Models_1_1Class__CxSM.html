Detailed Description

The Class_CxSM class Implementation of the CxSM as shown in the manual of BSMPT v2.

  • \( -L_S = msq/2 H^\dagger H + (\lambda/4) (H^\dagger H)^2 + (\delta2 / 2) (H^\dagger H) | S | ^2 + ( b2 / 2) | S |^2 + ( d2 / 4) |S|^4 + ( ( b1 / 4) S^2 + a1 S + c.c.) \)
  • \( -L_Y = \overline{U}_L V \text{diag}(y_d,y_s,y_b) D_R H^+ + \overline{D}_L \text{diag}(y_d,y_s,y_b) D_R H^0 + \overline{U}_L \text{diag}(y_u, y_c, y_t) U_R (H^0)^\ast - \overline{D}_L V^\dagger \text{diag}(y_u, y_c, y_t) U_R H^- + \overline{E}_L \text{diag}(y_e, y_\mu, y_\tau) E_R H^0 +\overline{\nu}_L \text{diag}(y_e, y_\mu, y_\tau) E_R H^+ + c.c. \)
  • \( L_G = (D_\mu H)^\dagger (D^\mu H) \)

with

  • \( H = \begin{pmatrix} H^+ \\ H^0 \end{pmatrix} = 1/\sqrt{2} \begin{pmatrix} higgsbase[0] + I*higgsbase[1] \\ higgsbase[3] + I *higgsbase[2] \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( S = 1/\sqrt{2}(higgsfield[4] + I higgsfield[5]) \)
  • \( D_\mu = -I C\_{}g/2 * W_\mu^a \sigma^a -I C\_{}gs/2 B_\mu = -\frac{I}{2} \begin{pmatrix} C\_{}gs B + C\_{}g W3 & C\_{}g (W1 -I W2) \\ C\_{}g (W1 +I W2) & C\_{}gs B - C\_{}g W3 \end{pmatrix} \) with the gauge base = [W1,W2,W3,B]
  • \( \overline{U}_L = \begin{pmatrix} \overline{u}_L & \overline{c}_L & \overline{t}_L \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( U_R = \begin{pmatrix} u_R \\ c_R \\ t_R \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( \overline{D}_L = \begin{pmatrix} \overline{d}_L & \overline{s}_L & \overline{b}_L \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( D_R = \begin{pmatrix} d_R \\ s_R \\ b_R \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( \overline{E}_L = \begin{pmatrix} \overline{e}_L & \overline{\mu}_L & \overline{\tau}_L \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( E_R = \begin{pmatrix} e_R \\ \mu_R \\ \tau_R \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( \overline{\nu}_L = \begin{pmatrix} \overline{\nu}_{e,L} & \overline{\nu}_{\mu,L} & \overline{\nu}_{\tau,L} \end{pmatrix} \)

The basis of the quarks given by \( [u_R, c_R, t_R, d_R, s_R, b_R, \overline{u}_L, \overline{c}_L, \overline{t}_L, \overline{d}_L, \overline{s}_L, \overline{b}_L ] \) and for the leptons \( [\overline{e}_L, e_R, \overline{\mu}_L , \mu_R, \overline{\tau}_L, \tau_R, \overline{\nu}_{e,L}, \overline{\nu}_{\mu,L}, \overline{\nu}_{\tau,L} ] \)
