Detailed Description

The Class_Potential_CPintheDark class Implementation of the model CP in the Dark as given in the manual.

\(-\mathcal{L}_S = m_{11}^2|\Phi_1|^2+m_{22}^2|\Phi_2|^2+\frac{m_S^2}{2}\Phi_S^2+(A \Phi_1^\dagger\Phi_2\Phi_S+ h.c.) +\frac{\lambda_1}{2}|\Phi_1|^4+\frac{\lambda_2}{2}|\Phi_2|^4+\lambda_3|\Phi_1|^2|\Phi_2|^2+\lambda_4|\Phi_1^\dagger\Phi_2|^2+\frac{\lambda_5}{2}[(\Phi_1^\dagger\Phi_2)^2+(\Phi_2^\dagger\Phi_1)^2]+\frac{\lambda_6}{8}\Phi_S^4+\frac{\lambda_7}{2}|\Phi_1|^2\Phi_S^2+\frac{\lambda_8}{2}|\Phi_2|^2\Phi_S^2 \)

  • \(-\mathcal{L}_{Y,\text{lepton}} = y_\tau\overline{L}_{\tau}\Phi_1\tau_R+y_\mu\overline{L}_{\mu}\Phi_1\mu_R+y_e\overline{L}_{e}\Phi_1 e_R\\ \) with \(\overline{L}_\tau = \overline{\begin{pmatrix}\tau_L\\\nu_{\tau_L}\end{pmatrix}} =\begin{pmatrix}\nu_{\tau_L}&\tau_L\end{pmatrix}\) for the third lepton generation (first and second work the same way)
  • \( \mathcal{L}_{Y,\text{quark}} = \overline{D}_R\text{diag}(y_d,y_s,y_b)V^\dagger \Phi_1^{+*} U_L+\overline{D}_R\text{diag}(y_d,y_s,y_b)D_L\Phi_1^{0*}-\overline{U}_R\text{diag}(y_u,y_c,y_t)\Phi_1^{+}V D_L+\overline{U}_R\text{diag}(y_u,y_c,y_t)\Phi_1^0 U_L\\ \) with \( U = \begin{pmatrix}u\\c\\t\end{pmatrix} \), \( D = \begin{pmatrix}d\\s\\b\end{pmatrix} \) and the Yukawa couplings \( y_q = \frac{\sqrt{2} m_q}{v_1} \). \( V\) denotes the Cabibbo-Kobayashi-Maskawa matrix. The VEV \( v_1 \) is defined down below.
  • \(\mathcal{L}_G = (D^\mu\Phi_1)^\dagger(D_\mu\Phi_1)+(D^\mu\Phi_2)^\dagger(D_\mu\Phi_2)\\ \) with \( D_\mu=-i\frac{C_g}{2}\vec{\sigma}\vec{W}_\mu-i\frac{C_{gs}}{2}\sigma_0 B_\mu \)

and

  • \( \Phi_1 = \begin{pmatrix} \Phi_1^+ \\ \Phi_1^0 \end{pmatrix} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix} \rho_1+i\eta_1\\ \zeta_1+\omega_1+i\Psi_1 \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( \Phi_2 = \begin{pmatrix} \Phi_2^+ \\ \Phi_2^0 \end{pmatrix} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix} \rho_2 + \omega_\text{CB} + i \eta_2 \\ \zeta_2 + \omega_2 + i (\Psi_2 + \omega_\text{CP}) \end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( \Phi_S = \zeta_S+\omega_S \)

using the configurations

  • \(\text{higgsbase} = \{\rho_1,\eta_1,\rho_2,\eta_2,\zeta_1,\Psi_1,\zeta_2,\Psi_2,\zeta_S\} \,, \)
  • \(\text{higgsvevFiniteTemp} = \{0,0,\omega_\text{CB},0,\omega_1,0,\omega_2,\omega_\text{CP},\omega_S\} \,, \)
  • \(\text{higgsvevZeroTemp} = \{0,0,0,0,v_1,0,0,0,0\} \)

and

  • \( \langle \Phi_1 \rangle\big|_{T=0} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix}0\\v_1\end{pmatrix} \) with \( v_1 \equiv v = 246.22\,\text{GeV}\,,\)
  • \( \langle \Phi_2 \rangle\big|_{T=0} = \begin{pmatrix}0\\0\end{pmatrix}\,, \)
  • \( \langle \Phi_S \rangle\big|_{T=0} = 0\,. \)
